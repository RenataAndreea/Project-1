Brooklyn Nine-Nine is an American police television sitcom that premiered on Fox on September 17, 2013. This TV series was created by Dan Goorand Michael Schur.

The TV series revolves around Jake Peralta (Andy Samberg), an immature but talented NYPD detective in Brooklyn's 99th Precinct, who comes into conflict with his new
commanding officer, the serious and stern Captain Raymond Holt (Andre Braugher).
